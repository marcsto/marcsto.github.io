Introducing Punch Corona (Alpha Release)

Marc Stogaitis


Punch Corona is a workout game where you get to beat up the corona virus. It uses machine learning to track your movements using your computer's camera. No data ever leaves your device to preserve your privacy.

It's a great way to spend your quarantine time or get back in shape after gaining the quarantine 15.

It detects your body movements from a simple webcam which means you don't need any special hardware like a Kinect or VR controller for a completely hands-free experience.

One pro tip: the most common issue is standing too close to the camera. Make sure to stand far enough back so that your camera can see your entire upper body and can still see your hands when they are stretched out punching up or down.

This is an alpha release, so you may encounter issues. It needs a pretty good machine to run smoothly as it has to run both the game and a machine learning model to track your body movements. Email me at marcsto@gmail.com to let me know if you encounter any problems or if you enjoy the game.

Check it out at https://marc.ai/punch-corona/
